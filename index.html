How It Works

Simple enough
to start today.

Pick Your Group

Join a Bangalore challenge — gym crew, running club, office team, or start your own. Challenges are small, local, and personal.

Live photo on the scale every week. No screenshots. No manual entry. Miss it, your streak breaks and your group gets notified.
