How It Works

Simple to start.
Impossible to ignore.

Step 01

Join a Group

Create or join a challenge group — gym crew, office team, running club. Set a duration (4, 8, or 12 weeks) and invite your people.

2✓
Step 02

Weekly Weigh-In

Every week, take a live photo on your scale. Metadata verified. No screenshots, no manual entry, no cheating. Miss it — your group gets notified.

Your % change, streak, and rank go public to your group every Monday morning. Social pressure, automated. The board waits for no one.
