Why FitBounty?

🏆 Competitive Fitness

Create a challenge with friends or coworkers and compete to lose the highest percentage of body weight.

🎁 Real Rewards

Winners earn coins which can be redeemed for fitness gear, supplements and vouchers.

📊 Weekly Accountability

Every Monday participants submit verified weigh-ins. Public leaderboards keep everyone accountable.

How It Works

1️⃣ Create a Challenge

Start a fitness contest with friends, office teams, or gym groups.

2️⃣ Weekly Weigh-ins

Participants submit weekly weigh-ins with photo verification.

3️⃣ Track Progress

Leaderboards rank players by percentage of body weight lost.

4️⃣ Winner Gets Reward

Top performers earn coins that can be redeemed for fitness gear.

Fair Play System

📷 Verified Weigh-ins

Scale photo verification ensures accurate weight submissions.

📍 GPS Activity Validation

Runs and walks are validated using GPS tracking.

🛡 Anti-cheat Detection

Suspicious activity is flagged automatically to keep competitions fair.
